Bio

I am a medical doctor by profession who is now transitioning into software development.

I completed six years of medical school, followed by two years as an intern doctor and one year of community service. While I value my medical training, I have always maintained a strong passion for software. This interest began in high school, where I took Information Technology as a subject. After completing my community service, I made the decision to pursue software development instead of immediately specialising in medicine.

My goal is to become a full-stack developer. This does not mean I will abandon medicine, but rather combine both fields. I want to build products that people can actually use, and I believe that my background in medicine and software places me in a unique position to approach problems from a different perspective.
